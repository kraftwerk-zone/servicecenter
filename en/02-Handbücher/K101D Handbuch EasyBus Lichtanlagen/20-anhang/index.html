    Appendix¶

    Naming¶

    Power Plant Type Master/Bus Node Inputs/Outputs Additional Information
    K Light Master 4 inputs / 16 outputs
    K Light Master 4 inputs / 12 outputs 500 mA per output
    K Light Bus Node 500 mA per output
    K Servo Bus Node 2 outputs
    K Motor Bus Node 1 output 10 A

    Types¶

    • Light
    • Servo
    • Motor

    Master/Bus Node¶

    • Master: Controls the system
    • Bus Node: Specialized tasks

    Terms¶

    • ControlPanel: Free PC software for configuring the system.
    • Control Pad: Pads mounted on the remote control transmitter for convenient control.
    • Parameter: Adjustable values and settings.
