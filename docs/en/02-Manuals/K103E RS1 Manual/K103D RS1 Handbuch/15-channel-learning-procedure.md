# Channel Learning Procedure

![](images/image\_013.png)

image8.png

Learning channels is crucial for proper operation of KraftwerK EasyBus systems. After any changes to your remote control settings (travel endpoints, directions, center points), channels must be relearned.

Servos and motors controlled by KraftwerK systems must be adjusted via ControlPanel, not the remote control menu.

1. Turn on the remote control and, if the drive motor is connected, elevate the model so wheels do not touch the ground.
2. Move throttle and steering sticks to their endpoints, then power on the model. The red LED lights up, indicating learning mode.
3. Return sticks to center position.
4. LED blinks to confirm each channel learning step:
   - 1 blink: Throttle full forward, then full brake.
   - 2 blinks: Steering full left, then full right.
   - 3 blinks: Auxiliary channel K1 full left, then full right.
   - 4 blinks: Auxiliary channel K2 full up, then full down.
   - 5 blinks: Auxiliary channel K3 full up, then full down.
5. When all sticks are centered again, the LED glows solid red, signaling the end of learning.
6. The system restarts automatically and signals readiness with double green LED flashes.
