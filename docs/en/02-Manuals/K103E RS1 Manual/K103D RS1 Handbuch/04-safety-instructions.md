# Safety Instructions

- Read this manual carefully, paying special attention to sections marked in red.
- Only perform wiring changes when the device is powered off.
- Ensure that unexpected reactions during startup or configuration cannot cause damage (e.g., keep drive wheels off the ground).
- Protect all electronic components from moisture and oil.
- Route data cables (antenna, bus cables) away from power cables (motor, battery, digital servos) to avoid interference.
- Replace any visibly damaged components or cables immediately.
- Operate components only within their specified technical limits.
- Disconnect the battery after use, especially important for LiPo batteries to prevent deep discharge.
- Avoid short circuits and reverse polarity connections.
