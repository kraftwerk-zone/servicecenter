# Sound Types

![](images/image\_042.png)

image59.png

- **Motor Sound Standard:** Acceleration and continuous driving.
- **Motor Sound Load:** Acceleration and driving under load.
- **Motor Sound Deceleration:** Vehicle slowing down.
- **Motor Sound Engine Brake:** Vehicle slowing with engine brake.
- **Motor Load Change:** Played once when driving state changes.
- **Motor Start/Stop:** Played when motor starts or stops; can be load-dependent.
- **Motor False Start:** Motor fails to start sound.
- **Turbo:** Turbo whistle sound.
- **Gear Shift Sound:** Played during gear changes.
- **Air Brake On/Off:** Sounds for air brake closing and opening.
- **Reverse Warning:** Played when reverse gear is engaged.
- **Horn/Horn/Fanfare:** Played via horn button, switchable.
- **Additional Sounds:** Custom sounds triggered by auxiliary functions.
- **Power-On Sound:** Played after module startup.
- **Start Sound:** Played when vehicle starts moving; usually unnecessary if enough standard motor sounds are present.
- **Stop Sound:** Played when vehicle stops.
- **MP3:** MP3 files can be defined; only one MP3 plays at a time.
