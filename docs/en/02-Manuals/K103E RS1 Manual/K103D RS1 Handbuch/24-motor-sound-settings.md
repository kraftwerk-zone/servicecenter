- **Motor Sound Presets:** Standard, Load, Deceleration, Engine Brake; checkboxes define active states.
- **Filename on SD Card:** Kraftwerk internal format uses .snd extension.
- **Play:** Play the sound.
- **Open:** Load sound files.
- **Volume:** Base sound volume, adjusted dynamically during playback.

![](images/image\_046.png)

- **Min RPM:** RPM at which sound fades in.
- **Reference RPM:** RPM at which sound plays at full volume without pitch shift.
- **Max RPM:** RPM at which sound fades out.

Each sound snippet fades in/out and pitch-shifts according to the RPM range.
