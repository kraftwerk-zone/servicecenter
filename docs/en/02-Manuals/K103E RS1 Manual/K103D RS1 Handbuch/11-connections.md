# Connections

- 5 servo inputs; the Gas/Data input also accepts IBUS/SBUS/SUMD signals with automatic detection.
- 5 configurable servo outputs (factory settings detailed above).
- OFF/ON/Standby switch and jumper for configuring sound and control modes.
- 6 EasyBus outputs.
- I2C expansion connector.
- Micro USB port.
- SD card slot supporting up to 32GB FAT32 formatted cards.
- Speaker output (4Ω or higher impedance).
- 10A switching outputs.
- Status LED.
- 4 expansion pins for plug-in modules.
