## Basic Settings

![](images/image\_034.png)

image52.png

- **Project Name:** Select projects, including preloaded sounds.
- **RPM Range Min/Max:** Defines motor sound RPM range.
- **Dynamics:** Defines how dynamically the sound reacts.
- **Motor Characteristics:** Sets max acceleration and deceleration speeds.
- **Turbo Characteristics:** Similar to motor characteristics but specific for turbo sounds.
- **Engine Brake Transition Speed %:** Speed of sound transition between normal and load states.

![](images/image\_035.png)

image53.png

- **Default Sound Type:** Preset sound type when adding new sounds.
- **Load Transition Speed %:** Speed of sound transition between normal and engine brake states.
