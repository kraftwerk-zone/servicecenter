# Product Description

The RS1 combines an electronic speed controller, sound module, and lighting system in one compact unit. It connects to up to five channels of any receiver and supports SUMD, IBUS, allowing up to 32 channels within the system.

## Robust Speed Controller

- Continuous current rating of 25A.
- 32kHz switching frequency suitable for bell-type motors.
- Includes clutch function and start assist.
- Support for gear shifting and cruise control will be enabled via future updates.

## Exceptional Sound System

KraftwerK sets new standards in programmability. While competitors allow only a few sound snippets for driving sounds, KraftwerK supports an unlimited number of sound snippets, organized as follows:

1. Define the RPM range (e.g., 500 RPM after start, 2300 RPM at full throttle).
2. Assign sound snippets to multiple RPM bands with minimum, base, and maximum RPM values.
3. At minimum RPM, the sound fades in with RPM adaptation; at base RPM, it plays at full volume without RPM adjustment; and at maximum RPM, it fades out again with RPM adaptation.
4. Overlapping snippets create an ultra-realistic soundscape.

There are four sound tracks in total: two for acceleration and two for deceleration. The first track is the standard driving sound; the second can be mixed proportionally as load sound or switched. The third track is the deceleration sound, automatically mixed in when releasing the control stick, and the fourth is the engine brake sound.

If this sounds complex, rest assured that six high-quality sounds are preloaded on the module. Additionally, a sound library with premium sounds will be available at [www.kraftwerk.shop](https://www.kraftwerk.shop), which can be purchased and downloaded via ControlPanel to your module.

## Unlimited Expandability

KraftwerK’s leading role in RC bus systems shines with the RS1. It features six EasyBus outputs for unlimited expansion. Lighting boards, switch extensions, servo controls, and more can be controlled via EasyBus.

A special feature is the four small connectors on the top side, allowing additional modules to be stacked. The first of these is the BL8A, an 8A brushless controller designed for hydraulic pumps.
