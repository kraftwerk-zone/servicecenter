# Installation on the Sound Module

1. **Preparation:** Make sure your sound module is turned on, operational, and connected to the PC.
2. The sound module requires at least firmware version **2024.3** or higher.
3. After startup, the module is automatically detected and displayed in the program.

![](images/image\_003.png)

image3.png

## Open Sound Settings

Open the sound settings in the program.

![](images/image\_004.png)

image4.png

Then click the **“Premium Sounds”** button to launch the premium sound manager.

![](images/image\_005.png)

image5.png

  
![](images/image\_006.png)

image6.png

## Login and Select Sounds

Enter your user credentials with which you purchased the premium sounds. After clicking **“Login”**, all your purchases will be displayed.

![](images/image\_007.png)

image7.png

In the right column, you can see the license status:

- **“Available”**: The sound is not yet assigned to any sound module and can be installed.
- **Serial Number**: The sound is already assigned to a sound module.

Select the desired sound, choose the appropriate project on the SD card, and click **“Download to Module”**.

In the example below, the module is already registered, but the sound is to be installed again, e.g., because the SD card was replaced.

![](images/image\_008.png)

image8.png

## Confirm Download

Confirm the download to start the process.

![](images/image\_009.png)

image9.png

After confirmation, the selected sound will be downloaded. The license file will either be newly generated or reused and then copied together with the sound to the sound module.

![](images/image\_010.png)

image10.png

## Completion of Installation

After the copying process, the sound will be automatically reloaded.

![](images/image\_011.png)

image11.png

The sound is now ready for use and can be used immediately.
