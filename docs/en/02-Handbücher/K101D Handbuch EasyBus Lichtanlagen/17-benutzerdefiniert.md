# Custom

Every channel except throttle and steering can be custom assigned. K1 and K2 with full and half throws, K3 to K12 only with full throws.

![](images/image\_041.png)

image69.png

 ![](images/image\_042.png)

"Duration" means the function remains active as long as the stick is in the position. "Memory short" and "Memory long" are for short- and long-pressed functions.

Available functions include lighting functions, horn, supports, tipper, ramp, starting, gear shifting, servo1/2, and much more.
