# Troubleshooting

## General

- **Turn signals and cornering lights respond reversed:** Channels are not learned.
- **Reverse light stays on when driving forward:** Channels are not learned.
- **Steering servo rotates incorrectly:** Correct rotation direction after learning with ControlPanel. Changes in the transmitter are overwritten during learning.
- **Light functions cannot be controlled:** Check if the model is operational (battery charged, transmitter and model switched on). Check wiring. Test the speed controller directly on the receiver without the light assistant.
- **No response after switching on:** Green LED should blink twice briefly. Channels must be learned.
- **Learning channels not possible:** Check connection and function of all channels with a servo. Use the LiveData assistant to verify channel movements.
- **No connection to PC:** Check driver installation, model switched on and USB connected, correct COM port selected.
- **Module not found after update:** Close ControlPanel, restart system, try update again. If problems persist, disconnect all modules except the affected one.
