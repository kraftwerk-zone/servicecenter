# Pad Light & Sound

Controls functions depending on key press with deflections from +100 to -100%. Connection as with basic/additional functions.

![](images/image\_038.png)

image65.png

Functions for short and long presses are color-coded and symbolically marked.

Special features: Some keys (e.g. headlight flasher, horn, supports) are activated by a long press and can then be controlled with short presses without triggering the short assignment. After two seconds of inactivity, the original assignment is restored.

Example: Press and hold the headlight flasher button → headlight flasher on, release → headlight flasher off, short presses toggle the headlight flasher on/off, after two seconds without pressing the mode resets.

The button for the additional layer switches to a second function layer, which is automatically reset after two seconds.

The behavior of the "All On/All Off" button can be configured in the system settings.

The Servo 1/2 buttons do not directly control servos but switching states that can be used on the servo outputs.
