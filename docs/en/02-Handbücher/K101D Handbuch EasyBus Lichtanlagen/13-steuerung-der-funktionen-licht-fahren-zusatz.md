# Control of Functions Light – Driving – Additional

The standard control is via four proportional channels (Throttle left, Steering right). Channel K2 switches between the groups Light, Driving, and Additional functions.

## Learning the Channels

1. Throttle – Brake  
2. Left – Right  
3. K1 left – right  
4. K2 up – down

## Switching Light Functions

- Parking light, low beam: K2 up + tap K1 briefly left  
- High beam: K2 up + tap K1 briefly right (only when parking light is on)  
- Fog lights, rear fog light: K2 up + tap K1 long right (only when parking light is on)  
- Turn signals left/right: K2 middle + tap K1 briefly left/right  
- Hazard lights: K2 middle + tap K1 long right  
- Flash-to-pass: K2 middle + tap K1 long left (then K1 left for On/Off)  
- Additional functions (full/half, short/long): K2 down + move K1 accordingly
