# Product Description

The KLM 4/16 is connected to up to four channels of the receiver:

- Throttle channel for controlling reverse and brake lights
- Steering channel for cornering lights and automatic turn signal reset
- Up to two additional channels for lighting and auxiliary functions

Alternatively, the KLM 4/16 can be controlled via CPPM, I-BUS, or S-BUS (if supported by the receiver) or a data interface.

Of the 16 switching outputs, eight are high-current outputs (up to 500 mA), the other eight have 180-ohm resistors and can directly drive LEDs.

Each output can be freely configured in function (e.g., parking light, brake light, turn signal) and behavior (switch-on/off speed, brightness). Output 8 can be configured as an infrared transmitter (transmitting diode included).

The four servo outputs can also be adjusted in rotation direction, end positions, and speed.

The KLM 4/16 is compatible with all speed controllers, sound modules, and remote control systems. It supports multiswitch modules from Graupner and Robbe as well as our control pads basic and additional functions and the new Light And Sound Pad.
