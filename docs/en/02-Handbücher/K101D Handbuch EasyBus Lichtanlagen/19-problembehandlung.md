# Troubleshooting

## General

- **Turn signals and cornering lights respond reversed:** Channels are not calibrated.
- **Reverse light is on when driving forward:** Channels are not calibrated.
- **Steering servo rotates the wrong way:** Adjust the rotation direction after calibration with ControlPanel. Changes in the transmitter will be overwritten during calibration.
- **Lighting functions cannot be controlled:** Check if the model is operational (battery charged, transmitter and model turned on). Check wiring and connect the speed controller directly to the receiver to rule out errors.
- **Lighting system operational?** Green LED blinks twice briefly after powering on.
- **Channels calibrated?** Calibration is necessary to define end positions and rotation directions.
- **Calibration not possible:** Check connection and function of all channels with a servo. Use the live data assistant to verify channel movements.
- **No connection to PC:** Driver installed? Model turned on and connected via USB? Correct COM port selected?
- **Module not found after update:** Close ControlPanel, restart the model (disconnect battery), try the update again.
- **No damaged module displayed:** Disconnect all modules from the bus, restart ControlPanel, connect the problematic module and reconnect.
