# Updates

After connecting to the ControlPanel, available updates for bus modules are offered.

![](images/image\_062.png)

image6.png

Tips for a safe update:

- Always connect only one module and restart the ControlPanel.
- Avoid power interruptions during the update.
- Disconnect servos or motors during the update to avoid interference.
- Regularly restart the model and ControlPanel, especially during unusually long operations.
