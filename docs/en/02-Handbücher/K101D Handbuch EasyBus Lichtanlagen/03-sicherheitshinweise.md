# Safety Instructions

- Read this manual carefully and pay special attention to the warnings marked in red.
- Changes to the wiring may only be made when the device is switched off.
- Ensure that no unexpected movements (e.g., drive wheels) can cause damage during commissioning or configuration – ideally, prop up the model.
- Protect the electronics from moisture and oil.
- Do not route data-carrying cables (antenna, bus cables) parallel to power cables (motor, battery, digital servos) to avoid interference.
- Ensure a stable and sufficient power supply. Cheap BECs or speed controllers with "BEC 5V/1A" specifications often do not provide the necessary voltage quality.
- Replace damaged components and cables immediately.
- Operate the components only within the specified technical specifications.
- Disconnect the battery after driving, especially important for LiPo batteries.
- Avoid short circuits and reverse polarity.
