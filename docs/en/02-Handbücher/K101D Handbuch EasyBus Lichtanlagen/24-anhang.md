# Appendix

## Naming

| Power Plant | Type | Master/Bus Node | Inputs/Outputs | Additional Information |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| K | L | M | 4/16 | Master with 4 inputs / 16 outputs |
| K | L | M | 4/12 | Master with 4 inputs / 12 outputs, 500 mA per output |
| K | L | B | 8 | Bus node, 500 mA per output |
| K | M | B | 1 | Bus node, 10 A output |

## Terms

- **ControlPanel:** Free PC software for system configuration.
- **Control Pad:** Pads mounted on the remote control transmitter for comfortable control.
- **Parameter:** Variable values or settings.
