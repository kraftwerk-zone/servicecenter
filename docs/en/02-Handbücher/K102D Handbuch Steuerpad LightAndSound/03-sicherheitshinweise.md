# Safety Instructions

Please read this manual carefully and pay special attention to the warnings marked in red.

- Changes to the wiring may only be made when the device is switched off.
- Ensure that no unexpected movements can occur during commissioning or configuration (e.g., remove drive wheels from the ground) to avoid damage.
- Always place the electronics in protected areas, especially away from moisture and oil.
- Do not route data-carrying cables (e.g., antenna and bus cables) near power cables (motor, battery, digital servos) to avoid interference.
- Ensure a sufficient and stable power supply for the electronics. Cheap BECs or speed controllers with "BEC 5V/1A" specifications often do not provide the necessary voltage quality.
- Replace damaged components and cables immediately.
- Operate the components only within the specified technical specifications.
- Disconnect the battery after driving, especially with LiPo and LiFe batteries, to avoid damage and safety risks.
- Avoid short circuits and reverse polarity.

**Important:** Do not connect the control pad to a Hall sensor controller! Opening the remote control transmitter may void the warranty. If you have any questions, please contact the manufacturer.
