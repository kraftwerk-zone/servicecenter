# Sound Types

![](images/image\_045.png)

image59.png

- **Engine Sound Standard:** Acceleration and continuous driving
- **Engine Sound Load:** Acceleration and driving in load mode
- **Engine Sound Deceleration:** Vehicle is slowing down
- **Engine Sound Engine Brake:** Vehicle is slowing down with engine brake
- **Engine Sound Load Change:** One-time sound during driving condition change
- **Engine Start/Stop:** Start and stop sound, load-dependent switchable
- **Engine False Start:** False start sound
- **Turbo:** Turbo whistle
- **Gear Shift Noise:** Sound during gear change
- **Air Brake On/Off:** Sounds of the air brake
- **Reverse Warning:** When reversing
- **Horn/Horn/Fanfare:** Switchable via horn
- **Additional Sound:** User-defined sounds
- **Power-On Sound:** After complete module start
- **Start-Up Sound:** When starting off (usually not necessary)
- **Stop Sound:** When stopping
- **MP3:** Only one MP3 playable at a time
