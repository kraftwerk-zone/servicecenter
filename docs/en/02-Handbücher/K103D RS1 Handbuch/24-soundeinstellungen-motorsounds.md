- **Engine Sound Preset:** Standard, Load, Delay, Engine Brake
- **Filename on SD Card:** .snd is the internal Kraftwerk format
- **Play:** Play sound
- **Open:** Load sound file
- **Volume:** Base volume, depending on driving situation
- **Checkmark:** Define in which driving states the sound is active

## Sound Visualization

![](images/image\_049.png)

image63.png

- **Min RPM:** RPM at which the sound is slowly faded in
- **Ref RPM:** Base RPM at which the sound is played at full volume
- **Max RPM:** RPM up to which the sound is slowly faded out

The sounds are faded in and out according to the RPM, creating a realistic soundscape.
