# The Most Important at a Glance

## Software Version

Please always download the latest version of the ControlPanel and connect the module to your PC. All available updates, including new features and bug fixes, will be applied automatically.

## Status LED

- **Green (short blink every 2 seconds):** Normal operation  
- **Yellow (slower blinking):** Standby mode, no control signals are accepted  
- **Fast red blinking:** Undervoltage – hazard lights active, motor is shut off  
- **Slow red or other colored blinking:** Please contact our support

## Standby Switch

With the switch, you can turn the model on and off or put it into standby mode:

- **Standby mode:** All functions continue running, but no control signals from the remote control are accepted – ideal for demonstrations.  
- **OFF position:** Controller, servos, and bus modules are switched off. Warning: A small current flow may still exist, which can lead to deep discharge of the battery. Therefore, always disconnect the battery, at the latest at the end of the day.
