# System Settings

Basic settings that significantly influence the behavior of the model:

![](images/image\_032.png)

image47.png

- **Name:** Designation of the module in the overview, important when using multiple KLB modules
- **Learning mode active:** Manual learning procedure can be deactivated here
- **Cut-off voltage:** 6.4 V for 7.2 V NiMh/NiCd batteries, 9.6 V for 3S LiPo
- **Model number:** Switch between three models
- **Short/Long actuation:** Defines from when a button press is considered long
- **Volume:** Starting volume of the sound, automatically adjusts to potentiometer or stick travel
- **Half deflection and full deflection:** Threshold values for deflections
- **Infrared transmission power:** Setting depending on the distance of the diodes (1 = small, 3 = large)
- **Motor profile:** Influences starting behavior and cruise control
- **Motor start automatic:** Motor starts with slight forward movement of the stick
- **Motor stop after:** Time in seconds until motor stop due to inactivity
- **Daytime running light always on:** Switches on daytime running light before parking light
- **Turn signal On/Off:** Pulse duration for blink speed
- **Speed controller type:** Currently only "Brake separate" supported
- **Reverse driving:** Coupling of hazard lights and turn signals
- **Brake light sensitivity and afterglow**
- **Reverse light afterglow**
- **Steering type:** Automatic or manual turn signal operation
- **Turn signal threshold:** From when turn signals are switched on or reset
- **Cornering light type:** Always on or only with driving light
- **Motor runs immediately:** Motor starts immediately after switching on or manually
- **Party mode:** Defines functions on button press on the control pad
- **Pad light & sound:** Fine tuning of the control pad channels
- **Hydraulic speed increase and after-run:** Settings for hydraulic channel

## Additional Functions

- **Sound change:** Switch between sounds by button press
- **Model selector switch:** Switch between models (depending on system settings)
- **StandBy switch:** StandBy mode analogous to the switch on RS1
- **Volume:** Direct volume control via remote control
- **Hydraulics:** Triggers speed increase of the motor sound
