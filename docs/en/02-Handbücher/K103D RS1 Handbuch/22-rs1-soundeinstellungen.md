## Basic Settings

![](images/image\_037.png)

image52.png

- **Project Name:** Selection of projects and included sounds
- **RPM Range Min/Max:** Defines the engine sound RPM range
- **Dynamics:** Determines how agile the sound reacts to driving conditions
- **Engine Characteristic:** Maximum speed for acceleration and deceleration
- **Turbo Characteristic:** Maximum speed for turbo acceleration and deceleration
- **Engine Brake Switch Speed %:** Transition speed of the sound between normal state and load
- **Default Type:** Preselection of the sound type for new sound entries
- **Load Switch Speed %:** Transition speed between normal state and engine brake
