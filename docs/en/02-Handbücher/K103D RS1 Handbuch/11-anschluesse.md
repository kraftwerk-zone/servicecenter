# Connections

- 5 servo inputs – an IBUS/SBUS/SUMD signal can also be connected to Gas/Data (automatic detection)
- 5 servo outputs (adjustable, factory settings see above)
- Off/On/Standby switch
- Standby jumper for configuring sound and control mode
- 6 EasyBus outputs
- I2C expansion port
- Micro-USB port
- SD card slot (max. 32 GB, FAT32 formatted)
- Speaker connection (4 Ohm or higher)
- 10A switching outputs
- Status LED
- 4 expansion pins for plug-in modules
