# Product Description

The RS1 combines speed controller, sound module, and lighting system in a compact device. It can be connected to up to five channels of any receiver and also supports SUMD, IBUS, and other protocols, making up to 32 channels available in the system.

## Robust Speed Controller

- Continuous load capacity of 25 A
- 32 kHz control – suitable for bell armature motors
- Integrated clutch function and start-up assistance
- Future features such as gearbox support and cruise control will be unlocked via update

## Exceptional Sound

KraftwerK sets new standards in sound programmability. While competitors only allow a few sound snippets, the RS1 permits any number of sound snippets, flexibly defined over RPM ranges:

- Define the RPM range, e.g., 500 rpm after start-up up to 2300 rpm at full throttle.
- Assign sound snippets with base RPM, minimum, and maximum for any number of ranges.
- Sounds fade in and out adapted to RPM, creating an ultra-realistic soundscape.
- Four audio tracks are available: two for acceleration, two for deceleration (including engine braking).

For beginners, six high-quality sounds are already pre-installed on the module. Additionally, a sound library is offered at [www.kraftwerk.shop](https://www.kraftwerk.shop), where premium sounds can be purchased affordably and loaded onto the module via the ControlPanel.

## Unlimited Expandability

The RS1 features six EasyBus outputs for expansion with lighting boards, switch extensions, servo controls, and more. A special highlight is four sockets on the top, allowing additional modules to be stacked. The first of these modules is the BL8A, an 8A brushless controller for hydraulic pumps.
