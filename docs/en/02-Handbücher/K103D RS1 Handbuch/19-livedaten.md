# Live Data

The Live Data view offers a real-time visualization of stick movements and switch states. The channel assignment can be adjusted at the bottom left, especially when using IBUS, SBUS, or SUMD. Channels can also be individually learned here.

To learn a channel, click the "Learn" button and follow the instructions.
