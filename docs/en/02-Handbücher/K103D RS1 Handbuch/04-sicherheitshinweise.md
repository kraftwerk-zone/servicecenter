# Safety Instructions

Please read this manual carefully and pay special attention to the warnings marked in red.

- Changes to the wiring may only be made when the power is off.
- Ensure that no unexpected movements of the model can cause damage during commissioning and configuration (e.g., prop up the model, keep drive wheels off the ground).
- Protect the electronics from moisture, oil, and other contaminants.
- Do not route data-carrying cables (e.g., antenna or bus cables) near power cables (motor, battery, digital servos) to avoid interference.
- Replace damaged components and cables immediately.
- Operate the components only within the specified technical specifications.
- Disconnect the battery after use, especially with LiPo batteries, to avoid deep discharge.
- Avoid short circuits and reverse polarity.
